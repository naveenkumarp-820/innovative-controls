FAQ

Insights into our Solutions

Clear answers about our engineering capabilities, compliance standards, and digital transformation services.

Are your panels compliant with international safety and quality standards?

Yes, safety and quality are embedded in our engineering DNA. Our hardware solutions and panels adhere strictly to ISO, CSA, NEMA, IEC, and UL standards. Furthermore, our 6000 Sq.Ft manufacturing facility is geared to produce panels compliant with ISI, CSA, UL, and NFPA79.

Which industries benefit most from your automation platforms?

Our architecture is scalable across multiple verticals. We have proven success in Heavy Manufacturing, Automotive, Food & Beverage, Power Generation, Aerospace, and specific specialized divisions for Healthcare (RTLS tracking) and Smart Commercial Buildings.

How do you approach Energy Management & Sustainability?

We deploy intelligent monitoring systems that capture real-time energy consumption data. By integrating these insights with your central BMS or SCADA software, we help optimize usage, reduce your carbon footprint, and significantly lower operational expenditures.
